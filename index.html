Reach a validated score of 100% to win:
- Drag and drop items onto their corresponding layout slots, newly dropped items begin with a quantity of 1. When subtracting a quantity below 1, the item is deleted and the slot freed.
- Use your mouse left click (add) or right click (subtract) to manipulate slot quantities by 1. Pressing CTRL key changes the value added or subtracted to 5.
- Press Validate button anytime to check your current score and highlight errors.
- You can restart at anytime by pressing the Restart button.
